Mission

LabMate is still in development. This is what we are working towards...

LabMate is an Electronic Lab Book (ELB) web application designed to help you manage your scientific experiments and data. It provides a centralised space where you can document, organise, and access all your research activities.

Data Management

With LabMate, you can easily record and manage experiments, samples, and measurements in one place. The platform helps keep your data organised, ensuring you can find and use your information whenever you need it.

Reporting

LabMate includes tools for generating detailed reports and analysing your data. You can visualise your results, identify patterns, and make informed decisions based on the insights you gather from your research.

Integration

LabMate supports collaboration by allowing you to create organisations, invite other researchers, and share your data with them. Additionally, it offers API integration, so you can connect your existing systems to LabMate for storing and retrieving data efficiently.
